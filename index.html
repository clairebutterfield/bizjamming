About Claire

The Offering

Two half-days.
Two weeks apart.
A lot of clarity.

We'll spend focused, structured time together; enough to go deep, with breathing room in between to let the thinking settle before we lock in the path forward.

Session One

Dig In

We start high level — surfacing the problem areas and identifying the strategic pillars that form the foundation of your business.

Two Weeks Later

Align

We ensure your goals are clear and that everyone on your team is pointed in the same direction. No more crossed wires.

Session Two

Get Moving

We whittle it down to the immediate tactics to get you there. You walk away with a clearer path, and my number for any future support.
